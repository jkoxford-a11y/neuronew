üß† The Key Insight

You are the SAME person on Monday morning and Saturday morning. Same brain, same neurons, same you. But your subjective experience‚Äîyour qualia‚Äîis completely different. The only thing that changed was the neurochemical soup your neurons are swimming in. This is what it means to say that mood is neurochemistry experienced from the inside. When you feel anxious, that IS low GABA and high NE. When you feel motivated, that IS dopamine. The qualia and the chemistry aren't two different things‚Äîthey're the same thing described from different perspectives.
